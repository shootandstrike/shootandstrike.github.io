Our Store

Built by people who live the outdoors

Shoot & Strike is a South African hunting and fishing outfitter serving hunters, anglers and outdoor families across the country. We started with one idea — a single store that treats the rifle bag and the tackle box with the same respect.

Whether you're heading out for plains game at first light or chasing a line at the dam, we'll make sure you're set with the right gear.

  • Full hunting & fishing range under one roof
  • Hands-on staff who hunt and fish themselves
  • Scope fitting, zeroing & gear setup
  • Proven, warrantied brands — nothing that won't earn its place
  • Support for first-timers and seasoned hands alike
